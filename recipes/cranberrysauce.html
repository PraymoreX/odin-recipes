Cranberry Sauce Recipe

"The Cranberry Sauce Is The Essence Of Thanksgiving."

Cranberry sauce is a sauce or relish made out of cranberries. It is commonly served as a condiment or a side dish with Thanksgiving dinner in North America and Christmas dinner in the United Kingdom and Canada. Cranberries are naturally high in pectin, which is released when they are cooked with sugar and acid such as orange or lemon juice. The sauce can be made with only a handful of ingredients, including cranberries, sugar, water, and citrus.

Ingredients For Preparation;

  • 12 Ounces Cranberries
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Cup Orange Juice

How To Make Cranberry Sauce;

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve sugar in the orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the cranberries and cook until they start to pop, about 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. It will thicken as it cools.
  5. Enjoy!
